# User Guide — Patient

**Role:** Patient  
**Portal:** Patient Portal (`/PatientPortal/`)  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-24  

---

## Overview

Patients access Medyx HMS through a dedicated, secure **Patient Portal** at `/PatientPortal/`. This portal is completely separate from the staff system and provides self-service access to appointments, medical records, bills, prescriptions, and lab/radiology results. Patients cannot access any staff or administrative screens.

**What you can do in the Patient Portal:**
- View, book, reschedule, and cancel appointments
- View your medical records (OPD encounters, IPD admissions)
- View your prescriptions and download PDF copies
- View lab test results and radiology reports
- View and pay your bills
- Download receipts and reports
- Update your profile settings
- Get AI-assisted help

---

## 1. Getting Access

### New Patient Registration
Your patient account is created by the hospital reception when you first visit.

1. The receptionist registers your details and creates a patient account.
2. You will receive a `user_name` (your unique login identifier) and a temporary password.
3. Use these credentials to log in to the Patient Portal.
4. You will be prompted to change your temporary password on first login.

### Self-Registration (If Enabled)
If the hospital has enabled patient self-registration:
1. Go to the Medyx HMS Patient Portal URL.
2. Click **Sign Up** on the login page.
3. Fill in your details including a unique `user_name`, email, and password.
4. Your account is submitted for Admin approval.
5. You will receive a notification (email) when your account is approved.
6. You cannot log in until your account is approved.

---

## 2. Logging In

1. Open the Patient Portal URL in your browser (e.g., `https://hospital.example.com/PatientPortal/`).
2. Enter your `user_name` or email and password.
3. Click **Sign In**. You are directed to your Patient Portal dashboard.

> **Important:** If you see a login page saying "This portal is for patients only," you are on the correct page. Staff accounts cannot access the Patient Portal.

### Forgotten Password
1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your email for a password reset link.
4. Click the link and set a new password.

## 4. Appointments

Navigate to **My Appointments** from the navigation.

### Viewing Your Appointments
- All past and upcoming appointments are listed with: doctor name, date, time, type, and status.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Submitted, waiting for hospital confirmation |
| Confirmed | Appointment confirmed — please attend as scheduled |
| Completed | Visit has taken place |
| Cancelled | Appointment was cancelled |
| Rescheduled | Moved to a new date/time |

### Booking a New Appointment (Online)
1. Click **Book Appointment**.
2. Select your preferred doctor from the list.
3. Choose an available date and time slot.
4. Add a brief note about the reason for your visit.
5. Click **Submit Request**.
6. Your appointment is created with **Pending** status.
7. You will receive an email + SMS confirmation once the hospital approves it.

> **Note:** If the hospital requires you to call or visit to book appointments, this feature may not be enabled. Check with your hospital.

### Rescheduling an Appointment
1. Find the confirmed appointment in your list.
2. Click **Reschedule**.
3. Select a new date and time from the available slots.
4. Confirm the reschedule.
5. You will receive a notification confirming the new date.

### Cancelling an Appointment
1. Find the appointment you want to cancel.
2. Click **Cancel**.
3. Enter a brief reason (optional).
4. Confirm.

> Please cancel appointments you cannot attend as early as possible so the slot can be offered to other patients.

## 5. Medical Records

Navigate to **Medical Records** from the navigation.

### OPD (Outpatient) Visit History
- View all outpatient consultations with: date, doctor, diagnosis, and treatment plan.
- Click any visit to see the full consultation details.

### IPD (Inpatient) Admission History
- View all hospital admissions with: admission date, discharge date, ward/bed, admitting doctor, and final diagnosis.
- Click any admission to see clinical notes and discharge summary.

### Lab Results
- View all pathology/laboratory test results.
- Each result shows: test name, ordered by, date, result values, reference ranges, and interpretation.
- Values outside the normal range are highlighted.
- Click **Download Report** to save a PDF copy.

### Radiology Reports
- View all radiology/imaging reports (X-Ray, CT, MRI, Ultrasound, etc.).
- Each report shows: examination type, date performed, radiologist's findings, and impression/conclusion.
- Click **Download Report** to save a PDF copy.

---

## 6. Prescriptions

Navigate to **Prescriptions** from the navigation.

### Viewing Your Prescriptions
- All prescriptions issued by your doctors are listed with: date, prescribing doctor, medicines, and dispensing status.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Not yet dispensed by pharmacy |
| Dispensed | All medicines have been dispensed |
| Partially Dispensed | Some medicines dispensed |
| Cancelled | Prescription voided |

### Downloading a Prescription
- Click **Download PDF** next to any prescription to save it.
- The PDF includes medicine names, dosages, frequency, duration, and doctor's instructions.

### Important Note
- Do not alter your prescriptions.
- Always take medicines exactly as prescribed.
- Contact your doctor if you have questions about your medication.

---

## 7. Bills & Payments

Navigate to **My Bills** from the navigation.

### Viewing Your Bills
- All bills are listed with: bill date, service summary, total amount, amount paid, and balance.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Unpaid | Full amount outstanding |
| Partially Paid | One or more payments made, balance remaining |
| Paid | Bill fully settled |

### Making a Payment
1. Click on an **Unpaid** or **Partially Paid** bill.
2. Click **Pay Now**.
3. Select your payment method.
4. Enter payment details as required.
5. Confirm.

> Payment processing depends on the payment methods enabled by your hospital.

### Downloading Receipts
- After a payment is recorded, click **Download Receipt** to save a PDF receipt.
- The receipt shows: hospital name, bill number, your name, services, amount paid, payment date, and payment method.

### Exporting Bills
- Click **Export Bills** from the My Bills screen to download a CSV list of all your bills for your records.

---

## 8. Profile & Settings

Navigate to **Settings** or click your name/avatar in the navigation.

### Updating Your Personal Details
1. Click **Edit Profile**.
2. Update: display name, contact number, email address, and address.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

> If your date of birth, gender, or medical ID needs to be corrected, contact the hospital reception — these fields are managed by administrative staff.

### Changing Your Password
1. Go to **Settings → Change Password**.
2. Enter your current password.
3. Enter and confirm your new password.
4. Click **Change Password**.

> Use a strong password: at least 8 characters including letters, numbers, and a special character.

### Notification Preferences
If the hospital has configured notification settings:
- Toggle email notifications on/off for appointment confirmations and reminders.
- Toggle SMS notifications on/off for appointment updates.
- Save your preferences.

---

## 9. AI Assistant

The floating AI button (bottom-right corner) is available on all portal pages.

**What you can ask:**
- "How do I reschedule my appointment?"
- "What does this lab result mean?" (general guidance — not a medical diagnosis)
- "How do I pay my bill?"
- "Where can I see my prescription?"
- "How do I contact the hospital?"

**Important:** The AI assistant provides general guidance only. For medical advice about your condition, diagnosis, or treatment, always consult your doctor directly.

If the chatbot cannot answer your question, it will offer to connect you with hospital support.

---

## 9a. Live Consultation

If your doctor has scheduled a remote consultation, you can join from the Patient Portal.

1. Navigate to **My Appointments**.
2. Find the appointment marked as **Live Consultation**.
3. Click **Join Session** when the session is active (button becomes available at the scheduled time).
4. Your doctor will be present on the other end.
5. After the session ends, the appointment record is updated automatically.

---

## 10. Logging Out

1. Click your name/avatar in the top-right navigation of the Patient Portal.
2. Click **Logout**.
3. You are redirected to the Patient Portal login page.

> Always log out when using a shared or public computer.

---

## Getting Help

If you need assistance:
- Use the AI assistant chatbot (bottom-right button) for immediate guidance.
- Contact the hospital reception by phone.
- Visit the hospital's public website for contact details and information.
- Email the hospital at the address listed on your appointment confirmation.
