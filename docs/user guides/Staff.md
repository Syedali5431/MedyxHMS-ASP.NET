# User Guide — Staff

**Role:** Staff  
**Portal:** Staff / Admin Portal (`/`)  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-24  

---

## Overview

The Staff role provides general access to Medyx HMS for hospital employees who do not hold a specific clinical or administrative role. This includes administrative support workers, general operations staff, and any employee who needs basic system access (attendance, leave, notifications). The specific modules available to a Staff user depend on what has been configured by the Admin or SuperAdmin for their account.

**Key responsibilities vary by assignment but may include:**
- Viewing daily operational information
- Submitting and tracking leave requests
- Viewing payslips and payroll summaries
- Receiving and acknowledging system notifications

---

## 1. Logging In

1. Open the Medyx HMS URL in your browser.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Select **Staff** when prompted to choose a role.
4. Click **Sign In**. You are directed to the general Staff dashboard.

> If you cannot log in, your account may still be pending approval. Contact your Admin or SuperAdmin.

---

## 2. Dashboard

Your dashboard shows basic operational information relevant to your assigned modules:

| Widget | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Notifications | Unread system notifications |
| My Leave Balance | Your current approved leave days remaining |
| Today's Announcements | Hospital notices and announcements |
| My Recent Activity | Your recent system actions |

---

## 3. Attendance

Navigate to **Attendance** from the navigation (if access has been granted).

### Viewing Your Attendance
- The attendance screen shows your personal check-in and check-out history.
- Filter by date range to see a specific period.
- Each entry shows: date, check-in time, check-out time, and total hours.

### Attendance is typically recorded by:
- The Admin or Receptionist on your behalf, or
- A time-clock system integrated with Medyx HMS.

> If you believe an attendance record is incorrect, contact your Admin for a manual correction.

---

## 4. Leave Management

Navigate to **Leave** from the navigation.

### Submitting a Leave Request
1. Click **Apply for Leave**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Leave Type**: Annual, Sick, Emergency, Casual, etc.
   - **Start Date** and **End Date**
   - **Reason** (brief description)
3. Click **Submit Request**.
4. Your leave request is sent to the Admin for approval.

### Tracking Your Leave Request
- The leave request list shows all your submitted requests with their current status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Submitted, awaiting Admin review |
| Approved | Leave granted — your balance is reduced |
| Rejected | Leave denied — a rejection reason is provided |
| Cancelled | You cancelled the request before decision |

### Leave Balance
- Your current leave balance (by leave type) is shown at the top of the Leave screen.
- Each approved leave reduces the balance by the number of days taken.

### Cancelling a Pending Request
1. Find the pending request in your leave list.
2. Click **Cancel Request**.
3. Confirm. The request is withdrawn.

---

## 5. Payroll (Payslips)

Navigate to **Payroll** from the navigation (if access has been granted).

### Viewing Your Payslips
- The payroll screen shows your monthly payslip history.
- Click on any month to view the full payslip breakdown:
  - Gross salary
  - Allowances
  - Deductions
  - Net payable amount
  - Payment method and date

### Downloading a Payslip
- Click **Download PDF** on any payslip to save or print a copy.

> If you notice a discrepancy in your payslip, contact your Admin or HR immediately with the relevant pay period reference.

---

## 6. Notifications

Navigate to **Notifications** from the navigation.

### Viewing Notifications
- All system notifications addressed to you appear here.
- Notifications include: leave approval/rejection, account updates, hospital announcements, license renewal reminders.
- Click any notification to see the full message.
- Mark notifications as **Read** to clear them from your unread count.

---

## 7. Profile & Account

### Viewing Your Profile
1. Click your name/avatar in the top-right navigation.
2. Select **My Profile** to view your account details: name, email, `user_name`, role, and department.

### Changing Your Password
1. Click your name/avatar in the top-right.
2. Select **My Profile** → **Change Password**.
3. Enter your current password.
4. Enter and confirm your new password.
5. Click **Save**.

> Passwords must follow the hospital's security policy (minimum length and complexity as configured by SuperAdmin).

### Username Policy
- Your `user_name` is your unique system identifier.
- You cannot change your `user_name` — contact Admin if a change is required.

---

## 8. AI Assistant

The floating AI button (bottom-right corner) is available on all pages:
- Ask questions about your leave balance, payslip, or module navigation.
- The chatbot provides guidance based on approved help content.
- Clinical or patient data is not accessible through the chatbot.
- If the chatbot cannot answer, it will offer to escalate to a support contact.

---

## 9. Logging Out

1. Click your name/avatar in the top-right navigation.
2. Click **Logout**. You are returned to the login page.
3. All session data is cleared.

---

## 10. Messaging

Navigate to **Messaging** from the navigation.

- Send direct messages to colleagues and managers.
- View your inbox and conversation threads.
- Use messaging for quick coordination without leaving the system.

---

## 11. Download Center

Navigate to **Download Center** from the navigation.

- Access HR documents, policy manuals, and forms uploaded by Admin/SuperAdmin.
- Download the documents you need for your role.

1. Contact your **Admin** or **SuperAdmin** to request module access.
2. They can update your per-user module permissions via **Module Management**.
