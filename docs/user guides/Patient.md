# Patient Guide

## Login and Dashboard
- Login with Patient role.
- Redirect opens Patient Portal dashboard.

## Core Modules
- My Appointments, Medical Records, Bills, Profile.

## Tasks
- View upcoming and past appointments.
- Review reports and medical records.
- Track payment/bill status.

## Exports
- Download available records/documents from patient portal where enabled.

## AI Assistant
- Use the floating AI icon at bottom-right from public pages or portal pages for help and support details.

## Notes
- Patients have view-focused access and do not perform admin data edits.
