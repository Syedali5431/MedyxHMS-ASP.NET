## Login and Dashboard
- Login with Receptionist role.
- Redirect lands on front-office and appointment-related screens.

## Core Modules
- Front Office, Patient registration, Appointment scheduling, Billing intake.

## Tasks
- Register new patients and edit demographic details.
- Book or reschedule appointments.
- Create initial bills and forward to billing team for processing where applicable.

## Patient Tracking
- Track appointment status and payment progress.
- Help patients with report collection and visit follow-up.

## Exports
- Export appointment/patient lists in CSV/PDF where available.

## AI Assistant
- Use floating AI launcher for guidance on booking and front-office operations.

## Account Notes
- Receptionist accounts require unique `user_name` values during creation.
- New signup-based accounts stay inactive until approved by Admin/SuperAdmin.
