# Pharmacist Guide

## Login and Dashboard
- Login with Pharmacist role.
- Redirect lands on prescription and medicine workflows.

## Core Modules
- Prescription, Pharmacy inventory/dispensing, patient medication records.

## Tasks
- View prescriptions and dispense medicines.
- Update medicine-related records where permitted.
- Coordinate with doctors on prescription clarification.

## Exports
- Export dispensing or medicine tables as CSV/PDF when available.

## AI Assistant
- Use floating AI launcher for medication workflow help and navigation.

## Account Notes
- Pharmacist accounts require unique `user_name` values during creation.
- Signup-created accounts are activated only after Admin/SuperAdmin approval.
